Welcome, ranger-photographer! 🦒

This tool teaches you the craft of wildlife photography — how aperture, shutter speed and ISO work together — through hands-on practice with African wildlife scenes.

Your progress is saved automatically on this computer, so you can close this page and pick up where you left off.

🎓 Part of your Level 4 Nature Site Guide training

At LWEC, the camera is professional equipment. The skills in this app are woven into the Level 4 NGuide Programme:

🐆 Interpretation — anticipating an animal's next move is how you position for the Golden Shot, and how you keep guests safe.

🤝 Client care — elite guides coach their guests' photography: better sightings, better reviews, professional recognition.

🦏 Conservation — biometric photographs of tracks and markings feed research and anti-poaching monitoring.

🌍 Ethics — the pursuit of a photograph never compromises the animal or the wilderness. The sighting always comes first.

By graduation, an LWEC guide is not just a driver — but a professional storyteller.

How to use this tutor

1. Learn — work through the tutorials for your level in the 📚 Tutorials tab, and pass each short quiz.

2. Practise — open the 📸 Exposure Simulator and try to nail a sharp, well-exposed shot in every scene, from an eagle in flight to a leopard at dusk.

3. Follow the programme — the 🎓 Programme tab holds the college's full 18-week training plan, week by week.

4. Track — watch your badges and completion bars grow in 🏆 My Progress.
