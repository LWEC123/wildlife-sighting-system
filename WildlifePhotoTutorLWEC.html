🎓 Part of your Level 4 Nature Site Guide training

At LWEC, the camera is professional equipment. The skills in this app are woven into the Level 4 NGuide Programme:

🐆 Interpretation — anticipating an animal's next move is how you position for the Golden Shot, and how you keep guests safe.

🤝 Client care — elite guides coach their guests' photography: better sightings, better reviews, professional recognition.

🦏 Conservation — biometric photographs of tracks and markings feed research and anti-poaching monitoring.

🌍 Ethics — the pursuit of a photograph never compromises the animal or the wilderness. The sighting always comes first.

By graduation, an LWEC guide is not just a driver — but a professional storyteller.

📋 Your mandatory Digital Skill-Up module — how it works

This app is the Digital Skill-Up module of your Nature Site Guide Level 4 enrolment. Completing it is required — here is the path:

1. Independent study — work through the tutorial lessons here during your off-time between field training sessions. The app tracks your progress automatically, level by level.

2. Field practicals — you will be assessed demonstrating these techniques live: vehicle positioning for photography, and advising "guests" on their camera settings (the 🤝 Photography Coach lesson is your preparation).

3. Portfolio building — once you complete this module, move straight into the Adobe Lightroom for Beginners course to process your field shots into a professional portfolio.

4. Your career advantage — an NQF Level 4 tracking and guiding certification combined with photographic skill makes you one of the most sought-after guides for luxury lodges catering to international photographers.

In short: finish every tutorial, master the simulator scenes, and bring those skills to your field practicals.

How to use this tutor

1. Learn — work through the tutorials for your level in the 📚 Tutorials tab, and pass each short quiz.

2. Practise — open the 📸 Exposure Simulator and try to nail a sharp, well-exposed shot in every scene, from an eagle in flight to a leopard at dusk.

3. Do — pick a 🃏 Field Assignment card for your off-time: a real project with self-check, camera in hand.

4. Follow the programme — the 🎓 Programme tab holds the college's full 18-week training plan, week by week.

5. Track — watch your badges and completion bars grow in 🏆 My Progress.
