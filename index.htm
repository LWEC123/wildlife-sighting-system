How to Photograph Animal Tracks
  • Place an object for scale (coin, pen, etc.) next to the track
  • Photograph from directly above the track
  • Ensure good lighting - early morning or late afternoon light works best
  • If possible, photograph multiple tracks to show the pattern/stride
